Grey Wolf

A wolf's paw can be up to 6 inches long.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupis
  • Height: 80 - 85 cm (Adult, at shoulder)
  • Lifespan: 5 - 6 Years
  • Habitat: Typically found in deserts, grasslands, forests and artic tundras

The grey wolf is one of the world's best-known animals. It is a social animal and travels in a group consisting of a mated pair and their offspring.
